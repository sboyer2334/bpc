WHO WE ARE

BPC CONTRACTING is a full service general contracting company that has been designing, building and remodeling homes and commercial property in St. Louis Area and surrounding counties for over 15 years.


CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

BPC CONTRACTING is a full service general contractor that provides a wide range of construction services from the design stage through completion. We take pride in delivering outstanding, quality workmanship, on time and on budget, no matter the size of the project. Our dedicated staff, from designers to project managers to skilled tradesman, provides knowledgeable guidance and innovative ideas to our clients, adding valuable insight to each project.
